Company History

The business is proposed by STI College Baliuag Grade 12 IT students.
They came up with this type of business idea because they want to showcase their skills
and offer a service business by utilizing their programming knowledge and developing
a new product that will be new on the market.

Our Mission

To create a photography studio that is admired for its creativity and excellence
while also being accessible and affordable to all. We strive to make our work available
in a variety of formats and platforms to ensure that everyone can enjoy our services.
We believe that the power of photography lies in its ability to bring people together and
tell meaningful stories, and by utilizing the latest technology and creative approaches,
we can provide our clients with the best possible experience.

Our Vision

To become one of the most successful companies that
provides costumers a trusted, high-quality photography services, and maintaining
excellent customer relationship. To become one of those competitive company
regarding to the pricing and packages that caters to the local market,
while maintaining high-quality photography services to the clients.
